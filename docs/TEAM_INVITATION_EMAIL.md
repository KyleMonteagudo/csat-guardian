**CSAT Guardian** is an AI-powered Customer Satisfaction Risk Detection and Proactive Coaching platform designed specifically for Government Support Engineers (GSX). The goal is simple but impactful: **help our engineers deliver exceptional customer experiences by detecting satisfaction risks before they become problems.**

### Why This Matters

Every day, our engineers work tirelessly to support government customers. But often, by the time we receive CSAT feedback, it's too late to course-correct. CSAT Guardian flips that model:

> **"Shift from reactive CSAT recovery to proactive customer experience management."**

Instead of learning about a dissatisfied customer after the fact, CSAT Guardian helps engineers:
- Recognize early warning signs in customer communications
- Understand *why* sentiment might be declining
- Take action with specific, AI-generated coaching recommendations
- Turn potential detractors into promoters
